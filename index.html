About me

I am Jorge Amaya and I am a business intelligence developer. Most of my career I have worked in a manufacturing company in the mining sector, with a considerable global presence. The last 5 years of my career I have spent developing data analysis projects, mainly for the finance departments as well as the manufacturing department. I believe that data is an important asset for any company that considers itself a modern and efficient company. One of the platforms that I have used the most recently in business intelligence is Qlik, which is an end-to-end development tool for data analysis projects. The platform includes the Extraction-Transformation-Loading process, options for visualization design, as well as data distribution tools. Previously I also spent a few years working with the SAP suite where I did multiple developments in its native programming language known as ABAP.

I am currently taking the Code Bootcamp at the University of Utah because I have the desire to learn about other software development tools and could give additional value to my skills around Information Technology.

In my spare time, I really enjoy being with my family and friends to take advantage of the outdoor activities we have here in Utah and surrounding areas.
